\section*{Phase 10 Task 4 - Held-Out Phase 6 Boosted-Tree Completion}

\textbf{Phase 10 parent doc:} \texttt{Documentation/phases/phase10.tex}\\
\textbf{Task goal:} Rebuild the Phase 6 dataset at meaningful size, evaluate \texttt{LightGBM} against the required wallet-unaware baselines on non-trivial held-out data, and refresh calibration, thresholds, registry, and model-card artifacts from held-out evidence.

\section*{Canonical Command}
\begin{verbatim}
python run_phase10_heldout_model_completion.py --json
\end{verbatim}

\section*{Done Condition}
Task 4 is complete when:
\begin{itemize}
\item the held-out dataset is materially larger than the Phase 9 sample,
\item train, validation, and test partitions are all populated,
\item the boosted-tree model is compared against the required baselines on held-out data,
\item calibration, threshold, registry, model-card, and shadow-score artifacts are written, and
\item the held-out baseline assessment is explicit rather than train-only.
\end{itemize}
